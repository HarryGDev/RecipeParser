White Peach Sangría Recipe

Adapted from "101 Sangrias & Pitcher Drinks" by Kim Haasarud
  • Email
Saved 169 Save to profile 169

Difficulty: Easy | Total Time: 10 mins, plus chilling time | Makes: 6 servings

There’s no getting around the fact that white peaches and fizzy wine are a dainty and feminine combination for a summer cocktail. But be sure to serve this sangría over plenty of ice: It may look delicate but it can pack a punch.

This recipe was featured as part of our Wine Cocktails photo gallery.

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 (750-millliter) bottle Moscato d’Asti
  • 1 cup peach nectar
  • 3/4 cup crème de pêche (peach liqueur)
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 white peaches, pitted and sliced, plus more for garnish (optional)
  • 1 lemon, cut into half wheels, plus more for garnish (optional)
  • Ice, for serving
INSTRUCTIONS
Combine all of the ingredients in a 2-quart pitcher or punch bowl and stir gently. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours. Serve over ice and garnish with fresh peach and lemon slices, if desired.
